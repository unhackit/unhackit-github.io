About us

welcome to SECLOT

We are a distress notification and management platform.
Our core values are Information Sharing, Accessibility, Speed, Data and Value.

With this values driving our operations, we hope to reduce crimes and other distress situations to a state where is it can be accurately forecasted.

Vision: To reduce crime in Nigeria by 20% by the year 2020
Mission: To provide efficiency of security responses to distressed citizens by providing useful information with speed and precision
